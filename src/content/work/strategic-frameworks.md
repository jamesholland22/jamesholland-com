## What it is

Most strategy frameworks were designed for business school, not for the kind of fast-moving, creative work that agencies and in-house teams actually do. This guide takes 20 frameworks — from basic tools like SWOT through to more nuanced models like Jobs to Be Done and the Value Proposition Canvas — and translates them into formats that are actually useful in a comms context.

## How I made it

I started writing it for myself — as a reference I could use and share in workshops. It grew from a set of notes into a structured guide over about six months of on-and-off writing. Each framework gets a plain-English explanation, a worked example from communications, and a one-page template.

## What happened

Shared it with a few colleagues and it spread from there. It's been downloaded several thousand times and I still get messages from people who use it in their teams.
