## What it is

A fortnightly newsletter that treats marketing strategy as a discipline worth taking seriously. Each issue focuses on a single idea — a framework, a case study, a mistake worth examining — and tries to say something genuinely useful rather than just filling inboxes.

## How it started

I started it in 2022 as a way to think in public. I'd been writing long internal documents for years and wanted to test whether the ideas had any value beyond the organisations I was working with. The first issue went out to 40 people I knew. It's grown from there without any paid promotion.

## Where it's at

Currently at 2,800 subscribers with an average open rate of around 48%. I've resisted the temptation to monetise it — it works better as a way of meeting interesting people and keeping my thinking sharp.
