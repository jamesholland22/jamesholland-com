## The problem

Every time the agency pitched, someone spent two days rebuilding the credentials deck from scratch. The result was inconsistent, often out of date, and didn't reflect the full range of the agency's work.

## What I built

A modular system of case study blocks, each written to a consistent template and tagged by sector, discipline, and outcome. A simple assembly guide let teams select relevant modules and drop them into a master deck template.

## The result

Deck production time dropped by around 60%. More importantly, credentials became more accurate and more strategic — teams selected evidence that matched the brief rather than defaulting to the most recent work.
