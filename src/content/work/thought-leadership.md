## The brief

A specialist professional services firm had deep expertise that wasn't visible externally. They were losing pitches to competitors who had a stronger content presence, despite offering a better service. They needed a thought leadership programme that was sustainable, credible, and commercially useful.

## What I did

I audited the firm's existing knowledge and ran interviews with senior partners to identify their genuine points of view. From this I built an editorial strategy, a content calendar, and a production process that minimised burden on fee-earners while maximising the quality of output.

## The outcome

The programme launched with a research report, a fortnightly newsletter, and a LinkedIn publishing cadence. Within six months it had generated over 40 qualified leads and the firm had been invited to speak at two major industry events.
