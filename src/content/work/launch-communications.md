## The brief

A consumer electronics brand was preparing their biggest product launch in five years. They needed a communications strategy that could work across 14 markets simultaneously, with a consistent core narrative but flexible enough for local execution.

## What I did

I led the development of the global narrative and press toolkit, working with regional PR leads to identify what would resonate locally. I ran media training for the executive spokespeople and developed a tiered embargo strategy to maximise coverage on launch day.

## The outcome

The launch generated coverage in every target market, with the product featured in three national broadcast segments. The brand's share of voice in the category increased by 18% in the month following launch.
