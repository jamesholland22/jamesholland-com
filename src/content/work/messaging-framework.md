## The brief

A fast-growing SaaS company had a product that engineers loved but that sales struggled to explain. With a Series B just closed, they needed messaging that could support a push into mid-market accounts — where buyers are less technical and more risk-averse.

## What I did

I spent two weeks embedded with the sales team, listening to calls and reviewing lost deals. The pattern was consistent: the product was being sold on features when buyers needed to hear about outcomes. I ran a messaging sprint with the leadership team, facilitated a customer evidence session, and developed a layered messaging framework — from headline position down to persona-level proof points.

## The outcome

The new framework was adopted across the website, sales deck, and outbound sequences within six weeks. Early signals were strong: demo-to-close rates improved and the sales team reported that discovery conversations were "much easier to steer."
