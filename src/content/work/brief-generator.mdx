## <GraphicSticker name="lightning" color="orange" /> The problem

Bad briefs are the most common cause of bad work. But writing a good brief takes time, skill, and discipline — and in busy agencies, it's often the thing that gets short-changed. I wanted to see whether AI could raise the floor.

## What I built

A structured prompt system that takes a short problem statement and outputs a complete campaign brief in a consistent format: background, audience insight, the single-minded proposition, mandatories, and success metrics. Built in a format that integrates with the tools teams already use.

## <GraphicSticker name="sparkle" color="blue" /> The result

Tested with three integrated campaign teams. The consensus was that the output wasn't always right, but it was always useful <GraphicSticker name="plus" color="blue" size="sm" /> — it gave the team something to push back against, which turned out to be the most valuable part.
